About This Site

What ParalivesGuide.wiki is, who's behind it, and why it exists.

🎯 What Is ParalivesGuide.wiki?

ParalivesGuide.wiki is a fan-made, community-driven guide resource dedicated to Paralives — the highly anticipated life simulation game from Paralives Studio. We create and curate detailed guides covering every aspect of the game: from character creation and build mode to careers, relationships, and modding.

Think of this as your friendly, cozy corner of the internet where you can find clear, well-written explanations — written by players who genuinely love the game and want to help others get the most out of it.

🙋 Who Runs This Site?

This site is independently built and maintained by a solo fan developer who's been following Paralives since its early announcement. There's no big team behind this — just one person who loves cozy simulation games, enjoys writing helpful guides, and wanted to create a quality resource for the community.

All guides are researched, written, and updated manually (no AI-generated content here!). Every article comes from real gameplay knowledge, community insights, and official developer updates from the Paralives team.

💡 Why This Site Exists

When a new game launches, the first few weeks can be overwhelming. You're trying to figure out mechanics, optimize your builds, and avoid rookie mistakes — all while dodging spoilers and scattered Reddit threads.

ParalivesGuide.wiki exists to solve that problem. Our goal is simple: be the single best starting point for every Paralives player, whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned sim veteran looking to master advanced techniques.
