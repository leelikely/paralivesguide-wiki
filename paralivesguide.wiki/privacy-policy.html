Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ParalivesGuide.wiki ("the Website," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. By using the Website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) for users in California, USA.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors. ParalivesGuide.wiki is a static content website — there are no user accounts, no comment systems, no newsletter sign-ups, and no contact forms that store data on our servers.

However, certain third-party services integrated into the Website may collect information automatically:

  • Advertising partners (such as Moneytag and Google AdSense) may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads.
  • Analytics services (if enabled in the future) may collect anonymized usage data such as page views, browser type, and device information.
  • Cloudflare, our hosting provider, may process basic technical data (such as IP addresses) for security and performance purposes as part of their standard service.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the content.

Types of cookies used on this Website:

Cookie Type Purpose Source
Essential / Functional Basic site functionality (e.g., Tailwind CSS CDN caching) First-party / CDN
Advertising Serving relevant ads and measuring ad performance Moneytag, Google AdSense
Analytics (future) Understanding how visitors use the site Google Analytics (if enabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on this and other websites.

4. Third-Party Advertising

This Website displays advertisements served by third-party advertising networks, including but not limited to Moneytag and Google AdSense. These advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites (interest-based advertising).
  • Measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns.
  • Limit the number of times you see a particular ad (frequency capping).

Google AdSense: Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

Opting out of personalized ads: You can opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies through:

  • NAI Opt-Out Tool (US)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Google Ads Settings

5. How Information Is Used

Any information collected through third-party services is used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements to support the Website's operation.
  • Analyzing Website traffic and usage patterns to improve our content.
  • Ensuring the security and performance of the Website.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Data collected by third-party services (such as ad networks) is governed by their respective privacy policies. We may share anonymized, aggregate data that cannot identify individual users.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Right to Access
