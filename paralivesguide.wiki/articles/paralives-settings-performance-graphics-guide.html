Paralives Settings Guide: Best PC Performance & Graphics Optimization

Fine-tune every setting to get smooth frame rates and crisp visuals – no matter your rig.

📅 Published: May 22, 2026·🔄 Updated: May 22, 2026
💡 Tip: Don't just crank everything to Ultra. Paralives' most demanding setting is Shadows. Lowering it one notch often gives the biggest FPS boost with minimal visual loss.

🖥️ Essential Settings (Adjust First)

Before touching graphics, get these right:

UI & Text

  • UI Scale: Set to 110% – 120% on 1080p monitors, higher for 2K/4K.
  • Text Size: Increase separately if UI scaling isn't enough.
  • HUD Transparency: 70% – 80% keeps info readable without blocking the view.

Mouse & Camera

  • Mouse Sensitivity: 80% – 90% for precise object placement.
  • Mouse Acceleration: Off – essential for accurate building.
  • Camera Rotate Speed: 60% – 70% to avoid motion sickness.
  • Camera Zoom Speed: 50% for controlled zooming.

🎮 Graphics Presets (Tested)

⚖️ Balanced (Recommended for Most)

Target: Intel Core i5/Ryzen 5, GTX 1660/RTX 2060, 16GB RAM

  • Overall Quality: Medium
  • Shadow Quality: Medium
  • Effects Quality: Medium
  • Reflection Quality: Low
  • View Distance: High
  • Object Detail: High
  • Resolution: Native
  • VSync: On (if screen tearing)
  • Frame Limit: 60 FPS (30 for laptops to manage heat)

📉 Low-End (Playable)

Target: i5/Ryzen 5, GTX 1060/RX 6600 XT, 12GB RAM

  • Overall Quality: Low
  • Shadow Quality: Low
  • Effects Quality: Low
  • Reflection Quality: Off
  • View Distance: Medium
  • Object Detail: Medium
  • Resolution Scaling: 85% – 90% (don't go below 80%)
  • Frame Limit: 30 FPS

🚀 High-End (Visual Treat)

Target: i7/Ryzen 7, RTX 3060+, 16GB+ RAM

  • Overall Quality: High
  • Shadow Quality: High
  • Effects Quality: High
  • Reflection Quality: Medium
  • View Distance: Ultra
  • Object Detail: Ultra
  • Resolution: Native
  • Frame Limit: 144 FPS (match your monitor)
💡 Important: If you face stuttering in large houses or heavily decorated rooms, lower Shadow Quality first. It's the single biggest performance hog in Paralives.

🔊 Audio Settings

  • Master Volume: Your preference.
  • Music: 50% – 60% (beautiful but don't drown out sound effects).
  • Sound Effects: 70% – 80%.
  • Voice Volume: 60% (dialog is in Parli language, no subtitles).

🎮 Gameplay Settings

  • Storyteller Difficulty: "Dog Storyteller" makes your Para's life easier; switch to higher difficulties for more challenge.
  • Auto-Eat: On – Paras eat automatically while at work.
  • Need Decay Speed: Slow it down if you feel overwhelmed.
  • Twin Probability: Raise if you enjoy large families.

Settings aren't permanent. After game patches, revisit and fine-tune. The best config is the one that makes you comfortable.
