16 Must‑Have Paralives Mods to Transform Your Game (May 2026)

After three days of testing the Steam Workshop, I've handpicked 16 mods that fix every annoyance and make Paralives truly shine. All tested on v1.0.3 — no garbage, no conflicts.

📅 Published: May 27, 2026·🔄 Updated: May 27, 2026
⚠️ Always back up your saves before installing mods. Save location: %USERPROFILE%\AppData\LocalLow\Alex Masse\Paralives\Saves. Limit yourself to ~20 mods for stable performance.

⚙️ 1. Functional Mods — Fix the Core Game

🧠 Smarter Autonomy Overhaul · RealismMods · 47,200+ subs · ⭐4.9
Rewrites Para AI so they eat, shower, clean, and entertain themselves. No more standing idle for hours. Must‑have #1.
🖥️ Better Build/Buy UI · UIEnhancer · 42,800+ subs · ⭐4.9
Hex‑color filtering, bulk select/move/delete, favorites & recent items. Doubles build efficiency.
🚀 Unity Optimization Project · OptimizationTeam · 51,300+ subs · ⭐4.9
Boosts FPS 30‑50% by rewriting memory management. Essential for low‑end PCs.
💾 Quick Save & Load · QoLMods · 36,500+ subs · ⭐4.9
Faster saves/loads, F5 quick save, F9 quick load.
🩺 Realistic Needs System · RealismMods · 29,700+ subs · ⭐4.7
Needs change with activity; food nutrition, sleep quality, plus thirst & comfort needs.

🎒 Recommended Starter Packs

🔵 Builder (12 mods): above + More Building Materials, Scandinavian Furniture, Curved Doors/Windows, More Plants.
