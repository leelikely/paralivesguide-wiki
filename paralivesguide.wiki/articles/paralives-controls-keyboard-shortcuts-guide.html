Paralives Controls & Keyboard Shortcuts (Essential Guide)

Stop clicking through menus — use these hotkeys to build, socialize, and explore efficiently.

📅 Published: May 22, 2026·🔄 Updated: May 22, 2026

🧑 Live Mode Controls

Mouse

  • Left Click: Select Para/object, interact.
  • Right Click: Cancel selection, open interaction menu.
  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in/out.
  • Hold Scroll Wheel + Drag: Rotate camera.
  • Hold Right Click + Drag: Pan camera.

Keyboard

KeyAction
Q / ESwitch between household members
FFocus camera on selected Para
COpen Para's phone
IOpen Para's inventory
RReset current interaction

🔨 Build Mode Controls

Camera

Same as Live Mode: scroll to zoom, middle-click drag to rotate, right-click drag to pan. Press H to center view on the lot.
