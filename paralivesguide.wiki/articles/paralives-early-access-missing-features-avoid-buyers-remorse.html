10 Missing Features in Paralives Early Access (Avoid Buyer's Remorse)

Before you click "Buy", read this. These 10 highly anticipated features are not in the game at launch — and some won't arrive until 2027. Plus, honest purchase advice for every player type.

📅 Published: May 24, 2026·🔄 Updated: May 24, 2026
⚠️ Reality Check: Paralives early access is impressive, but it's not a finished game. Many of the features you saw in trailers won't be playable for months — or even a year. Know exactly what you're buying.

❌ The 10 Missing Features (Ranked by Hype)

1. 🏊 Pools & Swimming

Estimated arrival: Q4 2026 (Oct‑Dec)
The stunning infinity pool you saw in previews? Not available at launch. Swimming requires complex physics and animation work still in progress.

2. 🏚️ Basement Tool

ETA: Q4 2026
You can build up to 4 stories above ground, but digging down isn't possible yet. Basements will arrive alongside pools later this year.

3. 🐾 Pets

ETA: First half of 2027
No dogs, cats, or horses at launch. The pet AI and animation system is being built from scratch. All pets will be free updates when they release.

4. 🚗 Cars & Bicycles

ETA: First half of 2027
Your Paras will be walking everywhere for the first year. The open‑world traffic system is still in early development.

5. 🌦️ Seasons & Weather

ETA: Q4 2026
Day/night cycle is in, but there's no rain, snow, thunderstorms, or seasonal changes at launch. These will be bundled with the pool update.

6. 💒 Weddings & Large Parties

ETA: First half of 2027
You can propose and marry, but there's no ceremony event. Birthday parties, family gatherings, and other social events are similarly absent.

7. 🌀 Curved‑Wall Windows & Doors

ETA: Q4 2026
Curved walls are supported, but you can't place windows or doors on them yet. A technically challenging feature pushed to the end of the year.

8. 🌳 Family Tree System

ETA: First half of 2027
You can have children and grandchildren, but there's no visual family tree. Tracking multi‑generational relationships isn't supported yet.

9. 🎣 Gardening & Fishing

ETA: First half of 2027
Basic planting exists, but a full gardening system with harvesting, crossbreeding, and fishing won't arrive until next year.

10. 👶 Deep Parenting

ETA: First half of 2027
Babies age up, but the detailed parenting interactions — diaper changes, feeding, teaching to walk/talk — are mostly absent at launch.

📅 Critical Timeline Warning

According to the latest roadmap (May 22, 2026): from June to September 2026, the team will focus entirely on bug fixes and performance optimization. No new features will be added during these three months. The first content drop isn't expected until October 2026.

🎯 Purchase Advice by Player Type

✅ Buy Now — Builders: The build mode is already the best in the genre. Gridless building, curved walls, free scaling, and the full color wheel are fully functional. You'll get hundreds of hours of fun from day one.
✅ Buy Now — Modders: Workshop support is live and the modding tools are powerful. Community mods will likely fill many gaps (pools, pets, cars) within weeks.
⚠️ Wait — Life‑Sim Purists: If you're here for deep everyday life simulation — parties, holidays, pets, family trees — the current content will feel shallow. Wait for at least the Q4 2026 update, or ideally mid‑2027.
❌ Do Not Buy — Perfectionists: If bugs and missing features frustrate you, wait for the 1.0 full release (expected 2028). The next two years will be a beta‑like journey.

💡 Final Pre‑Purchase Tips

  • 🕒 10% launch discount (May 25 – June 1): $39.99 → $35.99.
  • 💾 Saves may be wiped during major early‑access updates. Back up regularly.
  • 🚫 No multiplayer — purely single‑player.
  • ⚠️ Cheats disable achievements.
  • 🍎 Mac requires Apple M2 or newer.
  • 📈 Price will increase at 1.0 full release (2028). Buying now locks in the lowest price.

Paralives is a labor of love from a tiny team. Be patient, and they'll deliver something special. But if you're on the fence, there's no shame in waiting.
