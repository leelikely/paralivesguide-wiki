📅 Published: May 23, 2026·🔄 Updated: May 23, 2026

🧩 Steam Workshop & Blueprint Basics

Paralives launched with full Steam Workshop support. No external tools needed — just subscribe to a blueprint and it's instantly available in your game. Blueprints include all walls, furniture, colors, and decorations — exactly as the creator designed them.

The Workshop also hosts Paramaker presets, furniture mods, clothing, terrain presets, and full saves.

📥 How to Download & Use a Blueprint

  1. Subscribe: In the Steam Workshop, find a blueprint and click Subscribe. Steam auto‑downloads it.
  2. Find it in‑game: Enter Build Mode (Tab), click the Blueprint icon (looks like a house plan).
  3. Place it: Select the blueprint, move the preview to your lot, click to place. Rotate and adjust before confirming.

📤 How to Upload Your Own Blueprint

  1. In Build Mode, box‑select the entire house.
  2. Click the “Save as Blueprint” button on the bottom bar.
  3. Give it a name, description, and click Upload to Workshop.
